Furnace

  • Warranty
    • Need to register furnace so warranty goes from 5 years to 10 years
  • Servicing
    • After installation, get serviced in 2 years (adjusted during installion for best performance)
    • Once warranty up (10 years) get yearly service
  • Filters
    • recommends using MERV 8 and changing more often (we can buy these at the box stores)
    • change every 3 months but check filter and if dirty change earlier
    • Filterete 1500 filters are more aggressive than the Kirkland 2200 filters (pleats are tighter)
    • People buy more aggressive (higher MERV 8) filters thinking they are better
      • They leave them in too long (since they paid more for them)
      • Filter is choking the furnace when you remove the filter and the furnace quiets down
        • The furnace is loud because it is sucking air around the filter (when it is clogged)
      • It is important to change the aggressive filters more often as they can get clogged even faster
