Chicken Souvlaki

  • 1 lb chicken breasts
  • 2T lemon juice
  • 2T olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt + pepper
  • 1/4 tsp cumin
  • 1/4 tsp oregano
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 english cucumber (peeled, seeded, grated)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup yogurt & mayo
  • 2 tsp murt & parsley
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • salt & pepper
  • dash tobasco
  • cut chicken into 1 1/2" cubes
  • combine ingredients & marinate 10 min - 1 hour in fridge
  • add salt to grated eve
  • place in colander & at over a bowl
  • allow to drain 15 min
  • squeeze dry
  • combine remaining ingredients
  • add cubes and season to taste
