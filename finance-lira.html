Buy a House

    • Unlock 50% of LIRA into RRSP
      • LIRA/LIF rules vary by province
    • Yearly transfer from LIF to RRSP/RRIF on tax deferred basis
      • LIRA/LIF rules vary by province
      • Transfer diff between min-max LIF payment to an RRSP/RRIF on a tax-deferred basis
      • Max each year is the GREATER of:
        • prescribed max set by CRA
        • your investments returns from previous year
      • Example
        • Person is 67 with $500,000 in LRA with a investment gain of $40,000
        • CRA prescribed minimum is 4.35% ( $21,750 )
        • CRA prescribed maximum is 7.67% ( $38,350 )
        • LIRA was up $40,000 last year
        • Difference is $18,250 : $40,000 - $21,750
        • You can transfer $18,250 to your RRSP or RRIF - tax deferred
      • Doing this every year converts a less-liquid asset (your LIF) into a more liquid asset (RRSP/RRIF)
