Investment that "dervices" its price from another investment.

A Notional Distribution occurs when a distribution is made in the form of units, and then immediately consolidated with the units held prior to the distribution, so that the total number of units held after the distribution is identical to the number of units held prior to the distribution

Shows as income into brokerage account and then same income out

Causes increase of book value - no change in # units.
