  • BMO : Closing costs
  • property evaluation fee ($300)
    • may be required by lender to determine the sellling price is reasonable
  • land survey fee / certification of location cost ($1,000-$2,000
    • if seller cannot produce an up-to-date survey or certificate of location, may require for financing
    • Title insurance may be accepted in lieu of a survey.
  • Title insurance ($400)
    • protects you against loss caused by defects of title to the property, including real estate title fraud.
  • Legal costs (min $500)
    • Services provided by your lawyer will include: conducting a title search, drafting the title deed, and preparing the mortgage.
  • home inspection / appraisal fees
    • Trained Eye (Ari Marantz from Free Press) - $500 + gst
  • Land transfer tax (Municipal and provincial tax)
    • provincial or municipal tax based upon the purchase price
    • land registration fee calculation
  • prepaid property taxes and utility bills
  • fire/property insurance
  • mortgage default insurance
