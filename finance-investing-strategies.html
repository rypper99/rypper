Take Aways:

  • Keep REITs in RRSP or TFSA otherwise record keeping a challenge in non-reg account
  • PWL: does 1/3 Canada and market cap for rest of world
  • Developed / Emerging market ETFS – be careful not mix+match
    • There are differences in classifications emerging + developed markets
      • Eg. South Korea: MSCI index (iShares) Emerging but FTSE index (Vanguard) Developed
      • Eg. Poland classified differently by providers
    • Mixing/matching indexes can lead to doubling up or excluding countries
    • Pick 1 provider for developed + emerging funds
  • Mark Seed rules: Limit 1 holding to 5% of portfolio & Canadian content at or under 50%
  • www.dividendwatch free up to 10 holdings 1 portfolio ($64/year – premium membership)
  • www.simplywall.st – learn about markets, free (5 company reports/month, 5 portfolio stocks, 5 watchlists)
  • Tax loss harvesting idea: VEQT down switch to BMO version and claim tax loss on VEQT – worth it?
